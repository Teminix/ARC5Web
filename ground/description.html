About this Website





This website has been created by Team ARC5 which shows the temperature of the user's water heater. The difficulty with regular household water heaters is that it is hard to determine whether the water is suitable for use as the time taken to heat is different depending on the weather. The users' water heater has a thermal sensor inside it which reads the temerperature and sends it to the user through the website. To use the website, please click the 'sign up' in the navigation bar above.
